After weeks of development, the **official MCA website** is live at [minecraftclubofamerica.me](https://minecraftclubofamerica.me).

This has been a long time coming. We've always relied on Discord as our home base, and that isn't changing — but now we have a proper public-facing home that represents what this organization actually is.

## What's on the Site

The website covers everything about the MCA in one place. You'll find information about our government structure, the leadership team, the history of the organization going all the way back to December 2021, and full server information including live status for Global Dawn.

There's also a news section — which is where you're reading this right now.

## The MCA News and Beyond

The news section is built to host multiple publications written by MCA members. If you're interested in writing for the site, reach out in the **#general** channel on Discord. 

## What's Next

The website will grow as the MCA grows. Nation pages, event coverage, legislative records — there's a lot we can do with this. If you have ideas for what you'd like to see, bring them up in Discord.
