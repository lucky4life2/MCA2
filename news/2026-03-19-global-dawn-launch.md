After months of planning, **Global Dawn** is officially live and open to all MCA members.

Global Dawn is our most ambitious server yet — a fully vanilla survival world built on a **1:500 scale map of the real Earth**. Every continent, mountain range, and ocean is faithfully represented. Your nation's geography is real geography.

## What to Expect

This server is a fresh start. No modpacks, no plugins beyond what keeps the server stable. Just Minecraft, the real world under your feet, and the MCA's constitutional framework governing everything that happens on it.

Nations are already forming. Borders are being drawn. The World Congress will be in session soon.

## How to Join

To get the server address, join our Discord and check the pinned messages in the #server-info channel. You'll also find the current nation map and a guide for founding your own nation.
