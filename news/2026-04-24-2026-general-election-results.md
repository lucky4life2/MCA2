The results of the 2026 Minecraft Club of America General Election are in, marking a historic milestone for the organization and a testament to the strength of the democratic system our members have built together.

## National Leadership

At the national level, **William Rector** has been elected **National President** and **Kyden Blake** has been elected **National Vice President**. They will lead the MCA forward in the year ahead.

## Syracuse Chapter

At Syracuse, **Sam Olsen** and **Spencer Pike** have won their chapter elections and will represent their campus in the MCA's growing network of chapters.

## West Point Chapter

In the closest race of the election cycle, West Point has elected **James Loose** and **Nate Williams** — winning by a margin of just one vote. Their victory is a reminder that every vote cast by every member carries real weight.
