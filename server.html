Rules

Server Rules

These rules apply to all MCA servers and are enforced under the Anti-Cheat Act of 2026 and the MCA Constitution.

  • 01No X-Raying — using modified clients or texture packs to see through blocks is strictly prohibited.
  • 02No Duping — exploiting glitches or bugs to duplicate items is illegal. Any discovered dupe glitch must be reported to the Administrative Branch immediately.
  • 03No Exploiting — auto-clickers, fly-mods, speed hacks, kill-auras, and any bypass of vanilla limitations are banned.
  • 04No Griefing — destruction of builds outside of declared, consented warfare is prohibited.
  • 05Zero tolerance for bullying, harassment, or discrimination of any kind.
  • 06Respect sovereign nation territories — do not build or raid within another nation's borders without consent.

Allowed & Banned Mods

Enforcement

Penalties

All enforcement actions are reviewed by the World Court. Any player placed on Administrative Hold must receive a hearing within 48–72 hours.

1st Offense Confiscation of all illicit items and distributed assets, plus a 48-hour temporary ban.
2nd Offense Permanent ban and forfeiture of all personal assets and distributed illicit assets.
Emergency The National President may issue an immediate Administrative Hold if a mass-dupe is suspected, pending a World Court hearing.
