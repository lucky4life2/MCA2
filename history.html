Origins

Early History

Founded on December 5th, 2021, in Minot, North Dakota by Aiden Skates, our organization began with what is now referred to as "The First Server." This initial server existed for approximately one and a half months, spanning from December 5th, 2021 to early January 2022.

Despite its short duration, two significant events during this time would profoundly shape our group's trajectory:

  • The First War — A conflict between the Dream Kingdom and rebellion forces that became a pivotal moment in our early history.
  • The Story of the Five — A foundational narrative highlighting the original founders and key individuals who shaped our organization.

Notable individuals during this formative period:

Following the conclusion of the first server, our organization entered a transitionary phase characterized by multiple short-lived servers. This period was marked by several important developments:

  • The establishment of Mount Muffin News, our first printed newspaper
  • The foundation of OPS (Over Powered Society)
2022

Realms

Following our transitionary phase, the organization experienced significant growth with the establishment of our first realm, United Craft, founded by William Rector in March 2022. This Minecraft Realm marked a new era in our community's development.

During the United Craft period, several noteworthy developments occurred:

  • The rise and fall of SHIELD, a security organization founded by William Rector that assumed many of OPS's operational responsibilities
  • A significant conflict between SHIELD and an opposition group known as "the rebellion," which ultimately led to SHIELD's dissolution

Key figures during this period:

Aiden SkatesGroup President
Brett GrokieMember
William RectorFounder of United Craft · SHIELD

In May 2022, United Craft evolved into the Chameleon Server, established by then-group president Aiden Skates. This server operated from May 2022 to October 2022 and witnessed several significant developments:

  • The establishment of Honey-Acre Farms
  • The Frooty-Looper attacks, a series of coordinated actions by Random and Aiden Skates
  • The founding of Eithiopia, a kingdom created by William Rector that would later heavily influence Innovation Craft
2022 – 2023

The Dormant Period & Renaissance

Following the conclusion of the Chameleon Server in October 2022, our organization entered a period of dormancy that lasted nearly nine months. This hiatus ended on July 31st, 2023, with the launch of Innovation Craft, marking a significant rebirth for our community.

Two pivotal developments characterized this transitional period:

  • The strategic shift to Minecraft Java Edition
  • A change in leadership, with William Rector assuming the role of President

This period of transformation laid the groundwork for our subsequent evolution into the current Java Server era.

A New Era
Innovation Craft was the first modded server in MCA history and marked the beginning of the organization's modern period — culminating in the ratification of the MCA Constitution on May 13, 2025.
