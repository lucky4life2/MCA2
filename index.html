About

About Us

The Minecraft Club of America is a national organization uniting players from schools and local chapters across the country. We run a modded Java server, hold regular events, and operate under a full constitutional government — the World Congress, the Administrative Branch, and the World Court.

The MCA welcomes everyone. No matter who you are, we'd love to have you. Our Discord is the main hub for server access, events, and announcements. If you want to be whitelisted and participate, that's the place to start.

We're governed by a Constitution ratified May 13, 2025 — complete with elections, a legislature, a judicial system, and an anti-cheat law passed in 2026.

Recent Legislation

Anti-Cheat Act of 2026

Sponsored by Congressman Silky of Ark and passed by the 2nd World Congress on February 1, 2026, the Anti-Cheat Act defines and prohibits X-Raying, duping, and exploiting across all MCA servers.

First Offense
Confiscation of illicit items and a 48-hour ban. A second offense results in a permanent ban and forfeiture of all assets. All enforcement actions are reviewed by the World Court.
