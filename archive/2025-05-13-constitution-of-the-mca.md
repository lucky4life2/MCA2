## What This Document Is

The Constitution of the Minecraft Club of America is the supreme law of the MCA. Ratified on May 13, 2025, it establishes the structure of the organization's government, defines the rights and responsibilities of all members, and sets out the objectives of the club. Every law, executive order, and court ruling must comply with this document.

## Preamble

The Constitution opens with a statement of purpose: united as builders, explorers, and visionaries from schools and communities across the nation, the members of the MCA established this Constitution to create a stronger, more purposeful club — one bound by justice, fairness, and equal opportunity.

## Article I — The World Congress

The World Congress holds all legislative power. Representatives are drawn from each recognized nation on the server, with seats proportional to each nation's active population. Representatives serve one-month terms. The Congress passes server-wide rules, regulates trade between nations, declares war, levies taxes on in-game resources, and confirms all presidential appointments. A two-thirds vote overrides a presidential veto.

## Article II — The Administrative Branch

Executive power is vested in the National President, who serves a minimum one-year term. The National President serves as Commander in Chief, enforces legislation passed by the World Congress, holds veto power, appoints World Court Justices with Congressional consent, and addresses the public four times per year. The Administrative Branch also includes the Council of Presidents — all Club Presidents from individual schools and chapters — who meet monthly with the National President.

## Article III — The World Court

Judicial power is vested in the World Court, which consists of one Justice from each recognized nation. Justices serve two-month terms and may be reappointed. The Court hears disputes between nations, cases involving server rule violations, constitutional interpretations, and impeachment proceedings. All members have the right to a fair trial, the right to present evidence, the right to representation, and the right to appeal.

## Article IV — Objectives and Education

The MCA exists to promote learning in history, politics, geography, economy, civics, leadership, and creative expression — all through Minecraft. Regular activities include building competitions, elections and debates, historical recreations, resource management challenges, and diplomatic summits between nations.

## Article VII — Rights and Responsibilities

All members enjoy freedom of building and expression, the right to form or join a nation, the right to participate in democratic processes, and the right to due process. There is zero tolerance for bullying, harassment, or discrimination of any kind — violations result in immediate administrative action, superseding all due process rights. Members are responsible for respecting other players' creations, participating in governance, contributing to community projects, and reporting violations.

## Article VIII — Checks and Balances

Presidential vetoes may be overridden by a two-thirds vote of Congress. The World Court may declare legislation unconstitutional. Justices may be impeached by Congress for misconduct. Emergency powers require a three-fourths Congressional vote and expire after one month. All Congressional proceedings are published publicly.
