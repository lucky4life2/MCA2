## What This Document Is

The Nation Recognition Act of 2026 is a piece of legislation passed by the World Congress granting official Recognized Nation status to seven entities within the Minecraft Club of America. It also revokes recognition from two former nations — Arkain and Iceland — that no longer meet the constitutional criteria. Recognition unlocks full rights under MCA law, including representation in the World Congress and the World Court.

## Key Points

- Seven nations are formally recognized: The Republic of Ireland, The South African Empire, The Australian Tree Cult, The Roman Republic, Cloudridge (Scotland), Syrup Guzzling Central, and The Empire of the Covenant
- Each recognized nation gains sovereignty, proportional representation in the World Congress, a Justice nomination on the World Court, and the right to declare war under MCA law
- Arkain and Iceland have their recognition revoked for failing to meet the requirements under Article V, Section 1 of the MCA Constitution
- Formerly held territories of revoked nations are considered unrecognized or disputed until reassigned under MCA law

## Historical Context

The authority to recognize nations is granted to the World Congress under Article I, Section 3 of the MCA Constitution, with national formation governed by Article V. This act, submitted April 4, 2026 as Reference No. 0001, represents one of the first major legislative actions formalizing the expanding roster of nations within the MCA's second server generation.
