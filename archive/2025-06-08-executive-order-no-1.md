## What This Document Is

Executive Order No. 1 was issued by National President William Rector on June 8, 2025, just weeks after the Constitution was ratified. It establishes the four core Cabinet departments that form the administrative backbone of the MCA. Each department is a Cabinet-level entity tasked with fulfilling the national objectives outlined in the Constitution.

## Department of the Treasury

Responsible for overseeing all in-game economic systems, currencies, trade agreements, and the allocation of both server-side and real-world funds related to MCA operations. The Treasury maintains transparent financial records, manages club fundraising, oversees budgets for real-world expenses such as server hosting and promotional materials, and assists in funding public works, community projects, and long-term server initiatives.

## The Creative Team

Charged with coordinating and approving major building projects, server aesthetics, cultural initiatives, and all creative aspects of MCA server development. The Creative Team leads the creation of official MCA modpacks and resource packs, oversees server-wide building competitions and exhibitions, and partners with the Department of Communications to broadcast player creations and upcoming events. The head of the Creative Team advises the National President on all future server endeavors requiring creative planning.

## Department of Communications

Responsible for managing all official MCA announcements, public relations, newsletters, and inter-club communications. The Department oversees publication of the official MCA Gazette and other digital media, coordinates messages between the National Administration and Club Presidents, and ensures all members are informed of modpack changes, server developments, and public events.

## Department of Administration

Responsible for organizational integrity, meeting coordination, recordkeeping, and operational efficiency across all branches of government. The Head of the Department of Administration serves as Chief of Staff to the National President, overseeing all other departments. All departments must submit monthly reports and draft a Departmental Charter within 30 days of establishment.

## Key Requirements

- All department heads are nominated by the National President and confirmed by a simple majority of the World Congress.
- Each department must submit monthly reports to the National President and the World Congress.
- Each department must draft and submit a Departmental Charter within 30 days of establishment.
- The Head of the Department of Administration serves simultaneously as Chief of Staff, coordinating the work of all other departments.
