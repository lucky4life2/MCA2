## What This Document Is

The Anti-Cheat Act of 2026 is a law passed by the 2nd World Congress of the MCA and signed by National President William Rector on February 1, 2026. It was sponsored by Congressman Silky of Ark in response to a growing threat to server economy and fair competition from item duplication and X-raying. It is the primary enforcement document for cheating on all MCA servers.

## Key Definitions

**X-Raying** — The use of modified clients, texture packs, or glitches to see through solid blocks in order to locate ores, structures, or other players' bases.

**Duping** — Exploiting bugs, glitches, or server mechanics to illegally create duplicate copies of items.

**Exploiting** — Using modified clients or unintended game mechanics to gain an unfair advantage over other players. This includes auto-clickers, fly-mods, speed hacks, kill-auras, and any exploit that creates items or bypasses world barriers.

**Administrative Hold** — A temporary restriction on a player's ability to move, interact, or modify their inventory. Used to preserve evidence and prevent further exploitation while an investigation or World Court proceeding is pending.

## The Unfair Advantage Test

To determine whether a modification counts as exploiting, the World Court and Administrative Branch apply this test:

*"Does this modification provide the player with information or physical capabilities that a vanilla player could never achieve through standard gameplay or skill?"*

**Permitted (Quality of Life):** Xaero's Minimap, Saturation and Armor HUDs, Shaders, performance mods like Sodium and OptiFine, and Litematica holograms for visual reference only.

**Prohibited (Exploits):** Free Cam, X-Ray, auto-clickers, Litematica Printer and Easy Place functions, flight mods, and movement hacks.

## Prohibited Acts

- No member shall engage in X-raying for any purpose.
- No member shall engage in duping. Any discovered dupe glitch must be reported to the Administrative Branch immediately.
- No member shall exploit unintended game mechanics, bugs, or software vulnerabilities to gain an unfair advantage.
- The distribution of duped or exploited items is strictly prohibited.

## Enforcement and Penalties

**First Offense** — Confiscation of all illicit items and distributed illicit assets, plus a 48-hour temporary ban.

**Second Offense** — Permanent ban and forfeiture of all personal assets and distributed illicit assets.

**Emergency Clause** — The National President may issue an immediate Administrative Hold if a mass-dupe is suspected. A World Court hearing must commence within 48 hours of any ban.

All evidence of cheating is submitted to the World Court for review. The Administrative Branch is authorized to use server logs and anti-cheat plugins to identify suspicious behavior.
